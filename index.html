Highlights

Core capabilities designed for clean depth and comfortable viewing. Every feature is built to help creators ship better 3D faster.

Per pixel parallax

GPU accelerated depth weighted shifting for precise stereo alignment and minimal artifacts.

Zero parallax tracking

Subject aware zero parallax to keep the focus natural during cuts and complex motion.

Edge aware masking

Suppresses ghosting by protecting edges with smart shift masks and pixel healing.

Scene aware dampening

Smooths rapid parallax changes to maintain comfortable depth across scenes.

Floating window control

Simple controls for windowing to reduce divergence and improve screen edge comfort.

Flexible export

Half SBS, Full SBS, VR180, anaglyph, and interlaced outputs for your target device.

The VisionDepth3D Method

A proprietary workflow for real time 2D to 3D conversion, tuned for cinema and VR.

  • Depth weighted continuous parallax shifting
  • Subject aware zero parallax tracking
  • Edge aware shift masking to reduce ghosting
  • Floating window stabilization
  • Scene aware parallax dampening
  • CUDA accelerated stereo warping
  • Depth of field simulation with pixel healing

FAQ

Does it run on AMD or Apple GPUs

Yes on CPU. GPU acceleration is focused on NVIDIA with CUDA and ONNX. Apple Silicon support is planned through Metal backends in the future. AMD GPU users can run on CPU or try DirectML when available.

Can I use my own depth model

Yes. Place your model weights in the weights folder and select it from the model dropdown. ONNX and PyTorch options are available.
